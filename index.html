About Us

At Zen Japanese Education Consultancy (ZJEC), we believe that education and cultural exchange open doors to a brighter future. Our mission is to serve as a bridge between Bangladesh and Japan, providing comprehensive support for students, professionals, and families who aspire to study, work, or build a life in Japan. Founded by experts with deep knowledge of both countries’ educational and professional landscapes, ZJEC offers personalized guidance, ensuring that every client feels confident and well-prepared for their journey. We understand that studying or working abroad is not just a paperwork process — it’s a life-changing experience. That’s why we go beyond basic services, offering cultural insights, language support, and career counseling to help our clients thrive both in Japan and internationally. Whether you are a student aiming to enter a Japanese language school, a skilled worker looking for new career opportunities, or a family seeking a better future, ZJEC is here to guide you every step of the way. Our commitment to integrity, transparency, and client success has made us a trusted name in Bangladesh and Japan.

Our Services



At ZJEC, we are proud to offer a wide range of services tailored to meet the individual goals and dreams of each client. Our team works closely with you to understand your unique situation and provide the right solutions. Here’s what we offer:
✅ Japanese Language Courses (N5–N3): We offer expert-led Japanese language classes, helping you prepare for JLPT levels N5 to N3, ensuring you gain the communication skills necessary for study or work in Japan.
✅ Student & Job Visa Assistance: From document preparation to embassy submissions, we provide full support to help you secure your student or work visa without stress.
✅ JLPT & NAT Test Preparation: Our intensive courses and practice exams prepare you thoroughly for the Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) and the NAT Test.
✅ Interview & Resume Coaching: We help you create a professional Japanese-style resume (rirekisho) and prepare you for job interviews, so you make the best impression on Japanese employers.
✅ Educational & Career Counseling: Our experienced counselors guide you in choosing the right educational institution or career path, offering personalized advice based on your background and goals.
✅ Air Ticket & Travel Support: We assist you with flight bookings and pre-departure arrangements, ensuring a smooth transition to Japan.
✅ Post-Arrival Support: Our help doesn’t stop after you arrive — we assist with finding housing, understanding local systems, and adjusting to life in Japan.
